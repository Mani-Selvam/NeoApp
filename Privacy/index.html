Section 01

Overview

What NeoApp is and what this policy covers.

NeoApp is a business CRM and follow-up management platform designed to help teams manage enquiries, staff activity, customer communication, reminders, and payment-related workflows — all in one place.

This Privacy Policy explains how NeoApp collects, uses, stores, and protects information when your organization uses the app, website, or related services. We collect only the data required to provide CRM features, maintain account access, improve platform performance, and support business operations safely.

📋

Section 02

Data We Collect

The types of information we may collect include:

👤

Account details

Name, email, mobile number, role, login credentials, and company or workspace information used to manage user accounts.

🗂️

Workspace records

Customer enquiries, follow-up notes, staff assignments, lead details, message records, and other CRM data entered by your organization.

⚙️

Technical data

Device identifiers, notification tokens, app usage logs, and diagnostic data required for security, notifications, and reliability.

If your team uploads profile photos, company logos, attachments, or other media, that content is stored only to support the features you choose to use inside NeoApp.

🔄

Section 03

How We Use Data

We use collected information to operate the platform responsibly.

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To store and sync CRM records across your team
  • To send reminders and service notifications
  • To improve app stability, support, and security

We may also use information to respond to support requests, generate business receipts or communication records, protect accounts from unauthorized access, and maintain core features such as follow-up reminders, team coordination, and workspace management.

🔐

Section 04

Permissions

Device permissions are requested only when needed for specific features.

📞

CALL_PHONE

Used only when a user taps to place a customer or follow-up call directly from NeoApp.

🖼️

Media library / Photos

Used only when a user uploads a profile photo, workspace logo, or other selected media inside the app.

🔔

Notifications

Used only for follow-up reminders, account alerts, updates, and other service-related notifications.

✅

The Play Store build is configured to avoid restricted call-log permissions.

🔗

Section 05

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

Information may be processed by trusted service providers used for hosting, authentication, notifications, payments, email, or messaging as required to operate the app. These providers are given access only to the minimum information needed to perform their services.

We may also disclose information when required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of NeoApp, our users, or the public.

🗄️

Section 06

Data Retention

Data is retained while your account remains active.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of record, operational needs, billing requirements, dispute handling, backup cycles, and applicable legal obligations. When data is no longer required, we aim to remove or anonymize it where reasonably possible.

🛡️

Section 07

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your stored data.

Our security efforts may include authenticated access controls, protected APIs, encrypted connections, logging, role-based access practices, and regular monitoring intended to reduce misuse, accidental exposure, or unauthorized access.

No system can guarantee absolute security. We encourage organizations to maintain strong account credentials and report any suspected unauthorized access promptly.

⚖️

Section 08

Your Choices

Users and organizations can request support for account-related actions.

  • You can request account or profile updates through the app
  • You can contact support for data or account-related requests

Depending on your account type and workspace settings, you may also request help with correcting inaccurate information, deleting data, closing an account, or understanding how your organization uses NeoApp in day-to-day operations.
