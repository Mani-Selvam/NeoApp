Section 01

Overview

Data control and deletion overview for Neo Groww CRM.

At Neo Groww CRM, we respect your data privacy and control. We provide clear, transparent options for users and organizations to request or perform complete account and associated data deletion at any time.

Whether you are an individual user looking to remove your profile, or a company administrator intending to dissolve an entire business workspace, this policy outlines the exact methods to request data deletion, what data is removed, and our retention policies.

📧

Section 02

Account Deletion Request

How to contact our support team to request deletion.

Users can request deletion of their Neo Groww CRM account and associated data by contacting our support team.

  • Email: Send your deletion request to info@neophrontech.com
  • Details to Include: Please include your registered mobile number or email address to verify your identity.
  • Scope of Deletion: All account-related CRM data will be targeted for deletion.
  • Verification: Once verified, the requested account and associated data will be permanently removed from our active systems.
⚠️

For security and verification purposes, support-assisted account deletion requests may take up to 30 days to process.

Section 03

In-App Self Deletion

Primary Administrators can delete or disable their company workspace directly.

If your role is the Primary Admin of your company, you can manage the status of the entire workspace and delete or disable it directly within the mobile application. This bypasses the support queue and triggers immediate action.

🗑️

Delete Company Account

Permanently removes your company, staff members, admins, enquiries, follow-ups, and all associated workspace records. This action is instantaneous and irreversible.

⏸️

Disable Company Account

Blocks login access for the entire company. Existing users are logged out immediately, putting the account in a suspended/restricted state without deleting the data immediately.

To find these options, navigate to the Profile Screen in the mobile app, scroll to the bottom, and choose either the "Delete Company Account" or "Disable Company Account" option. Double-confirmation is required to prevent accidental deletion.

🛡️

Section 04

Data Retention & Removal Policy

What happens to your data upon deletion.

When a company workspace is deleted—whether via in-app self-deletion or by contacting our support team—the following database entities associated with that workspace are immediately and permanently removed:

👤

Users & Roles

All linked user profiles, staff credentials, and administrator accounts associated with the company are permanently deleted.

📋

Enquiries & Follow-ups

All customer enquiry records, lead sources, products, assigned follow-up entries, reminders, and activity history are permanently deleted.

💬

Communication & Messages

All chat messages, WhatsApp configurations/history, custom template configs, email settings, template records, and notification logs are completely wiped.

💳

Billing & Payments

All subscription records, plan overrides, payment details, and support tickets for the workspace are permanently removed.

✅

Once deletion is finalized, the corresponding system caches (dashboard, enquiries, follow-ups, users, plans) are invalidated, and all session auth tokens are immediately revoked to log out all users.
