# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

This app is intended for business CRM and follow-up management.

## Data We Collect

- Account details such as name, email address, mobile number, and company information
- Enquiry, follow-up, staff, and communication records entered by your organization
- Device and diagnostic data needed for app security, notifications, and troubleshooting

## How We Use Data

- To create and manage user accounts
- To store and sync CRM records
- To send reminders and service notifications
- To improve app stability, support, and security

## Permissions

- `CALL_PHONE`: used only when the user starts a phone call from the app
- Media library/photo access: used only when the user chooses a profile or logo image
- Notifications: used only for follow-up reminders and service alerts

The Play Store build is configured to avoid restricted call-log permissions.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by service providers used for hosting, authentication, notifications, payments, email, or messaging as required to operate the app.

## Data Retention

Data is retained while the customer account remains active, or as needed for legal, security, and support purposes.

## Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational steps to protect stored data, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

## Your Choices

- You can request account/profile updates through the app
- You can contact support for data or account-related requests
